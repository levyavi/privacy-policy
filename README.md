# Privacy Policy for AdMob Apps

This repository contains a privacy policy template that complies with Google AdMob and Google Play Store requirements.

## Files

- **privacy-policy.html** - HTML version ready for hosting on a website
- **privacy-policy.md** - Markdown version for easy editing

## Setup Instructions

1. **Customize the Privacy Policy:**

   - Replace `[YOUR_EMAIL_ADDRESS]` with your contact email
   - Replace `[YOUR_ADDRESS]` with your business address (if applicable)
   - Update the "Last updated" date
   - Review and modify any sections specific to your app's functionality

2. **Host the Privacy Policy:**

   - Upload the HTML file to a publicly accessible URL (e.g., GitHub Pages, your website, or a hosting service)
   - Ensure the URL is accessible without authentication
   - Test that the page loads correctly

3. **Add to Google Play Console:**

   - Go to your app in Google Play Console
   - Navigate to Policy → App content → Privacy Policy
   - Enter the public URL where your privacy policy is hosted

4. **Add to AdMob:**
   - Go to AdMob Console → Privacy & messaging
   - Add your privacy policy URL
   - Ensure it's publicly accessible

## Important Notes

- The privacy policy must be publicly accessible (no login required)
- Keep the privacy policy updated as your app evolves
- Review Google's AdMob policies regularly: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
- Consider consulting with a legal professional for specific requirements in your jurisdiction

## Requirements Covered

✅ AdMob data collection disclosure  
✅ Google Play Store requirements  
✅ COPPA compliance (children's privacy)  
✅ CCPA compliance (California privacy rights)  
✅ GDPR considerations (international data transfers)  
✅ User rights and opt-out options
